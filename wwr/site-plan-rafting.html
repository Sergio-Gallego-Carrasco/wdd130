Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our rafting company is to provide guided white-water rafting trips, equipment rental, and other related services for individuals or groups who are interested in participating in the sport of white-water rafting. This can include trips on rivers with varying levels of difficulty, from mild to extreme, as well as instruction and safety training for our customers.

Audience

People who are looking for a new challenge: White-water rafting requires teamwork, coordination, and communication, so it can be an exciting challenge for people who want to push themselves out of their comfort zone
